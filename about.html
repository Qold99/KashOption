How It Works

From inquiry to delivery—clear steps, no surprises.

From inquiry to delivery with disciplined process control at every stage.

1. Inquire

Tell us what you want via WhatsApp. Share product links, photos, or descriptions. We’ll confirm availability and give you a clear quote.

Send product links, quantities, and preferred shipping lane. Our team validates supplier credibility and sends a clear quote.

2. Pay

Full payment secures your order. We use secure channels. Once payment is received, we purchase and prepare your items.

After payment confirmation, procurement starts immediately and goods are prepared for the selected freight channel.

3. Ship

We ship by air (3–5 working days) or sea (6–8 weeks). You get tracking details. We handle customs and documentation.

Air freight is optimized for speed, while sea freight supports high-volume value. Tracking visibility remains active throughout transit.

4. Receive

Your package is delivered to your chosen address in Ghana. Door-to-door, with no hidden import charges when quoted upfront.

Customs and local handling are finalized, then your order is released for pickup or direct delivery across Ghana.
